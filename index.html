About Me

I'm happy to say that I joined the Thinkful Bootcamp in September 2020! Although challenging, it's been so rewarding, and I hope to develop awesome software and apps in the near future. Great Lakes Surf Data

Can you surf in Michigan!? Surprisingly yes!...This app was built in mind for those Michigander day trippers and weekend warriors who want to chase the Great Lakes Surf with informative data. I applied HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery to get the job done, and worked with two API's to display the data (Google Maps JavaScript, Storm Glass). I learned a lot about scope in this project, and what it means to really execute on ideas.
